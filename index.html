About Us

Our startup aims to address the inconvenience and difficulty of tracking expenses associated with carrying multiple cards by developing a smart card that allows individuals to make transactions without carrying all their cards. With our app, users can load all their debit, credit, and membership cards in the smart card and easily select the desired card for a transaction. The selected card will be used to make the payment when the smart card is presented for purchase. The app also offers access to a centralized dashboard where users can view and track all their spending in real time.

Services

Our startup offers a revolutionary smart card and app that allow individuals to eliminate the need to carry multiple cards and simplify their financial management. With our app, users can load all their debit, credit, and membership cards onto one convenient smart card and easily set a default card to make payment. Simply present the smart card at the point of purchase, and payment will be automatically deducted from the selected default card. Our app also provides a centralized dashboard for real-time spending tracking, making it easy for users to stay on top of their finances and make informed financial decisions.
